# Day 3: Portal Wireframes - Ableka Lumina Compliance Portal

## Overview
The Ableka Lumina Compliance Portal is a web-based dashboard for compliance officers, risk managers, and administrators to monitor, manage, and analyze KYC/AML compliance activities across multiple jurisdictions (IN, EU, US).

## Design Principles
- **Clean & Professional**: Enterprise-grade interface with intuitive navigation
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Live dashboards with compliance status updates
- **Jurisdiction-Aware**: Multi-jurisdictional compliance management
- **Risk-Based**: Color-coded risk indicators and priority-based alerts
- **Responsive**: Mobile-friendly design for field compliance officers

## Core User Personas
1. **Compliance Officer**: Daily monitoring and case management
2. **Risk Manager**: Strategic oversight and reporting
3. **Administrator**: System configuration and user management
4. **Field Officer**: Mobile access for on-site verifications
